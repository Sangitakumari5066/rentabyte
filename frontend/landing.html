RentAByte connects buyers who need encrypted cloud storage with sellers who rent out spare Dropbox space — all settled on-chain with POL.

Launch App View on GitHub
SEC
End-to-End Encrypted
Files are Fernet-encrypted before leaving your browser and split into 5 MB chunks.
POL
On-Chain Payments
Buyers pay POL to a smart contract. Sellers earn rewards per 100 MB contributed.
DST
Distributed Storage
Chunks are spread across seller-contributed Dropbox spaces for redundancy.
ACC
Instant Access
Download any file instantly — chunks are reassembled and decrypted on demand.
